Search behavior
Koreans don't Google — they Naver.
Over 60% of Korean local searches happen on Naver, not Google. If your business isn't registered on Naver Place, most Korean customers will never find you. We register and optimize your Naver presence so you show up where Koreans actually search.
Communication
Koreans message on KakaoTalk, not email or DMs.
When Korean customers want to book or ask a question, they expect a KakaoTalk channel. Without it, you're losing potential customers who won't bother with Instagram DMs or phone calls. We connect your ads directly to KakaoTalk inquiries.

Before trying a new place, Koreans search for Naver blog reviews and check ratings — it's how trust works here. We register your business on Naver Place so you're findable. Blog reviews come naturally from your customers over time — but without Naver Place, there's nowhere for that trust to live.
